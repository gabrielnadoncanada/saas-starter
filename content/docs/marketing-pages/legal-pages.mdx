Legal pages live in the **marketing** route group so they share the same header and footer as the landing site.

## Routes

| URL | File |
| --- | --- |
| `/terms` | `app/(marketing)/terms/page.tsx` |
| `/privacy` | `app/(marketing)/privacy/page.tsx` |

`MarketingFooter` links to both routes in its Legal column.

## Customization

Replace the placeholder paragraphs with your real legal text. You can keep the `main` wrapper and max-width layout, or split content into subsections with additional headings and lists. Update `generateMetadata` in each file for accurate `title` and `description` in search results and browser tabs.

> **Note:** These files are **not** a substitute for legal review. Use counsel-approved copy before production launch.
