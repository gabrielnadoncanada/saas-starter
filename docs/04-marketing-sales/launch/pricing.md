# Pricing

## Purpose
Define the launch pricing ladder and the feature split between plans.

## Recommended launch pricing
### Starter — 149 USD
For solo founders and pragmatic builders who want a serious base at the lowest acceptable price point.

### Pro — 299 USD
For buyers who want the best version of the product and lower setup friction.

### Agency / Extended — 599 USD
For agencies, consultants, and commercial client delivery use cases.

## Why this ladder
- 149 USD protects the premium floor
- 299 USD becomes the economic center of gravity
- 599 USD captures higher-value commercial buyers without custom sales friction

## Starter includes
- full starter codebase
- auth foundation
- protected dashboard foundation
- Prisma/Postgres setup baseline
- Stripe-ready billing foundation
- setup guide
- customization guide
- core landing and pricing page baseline
- future minor updates for a defined launch window

## Pro includes
- everything in Starter
- better docs and faster onboarding guidance
- premium UI sections or additional polished app blocks
- advanced examples for common SaaS customization paths
- higher perceived value pack for serious builders
- priority updates or broader update window

## Agency / Extended includes
- everything in Pro
- broader commercial usage rights
- client project usage
- agency-safe licensing
- optional light priority support or onboarding bonus

## Pricing rules
- never go below 149 USD
- do not cripple Starter to force Pro
- make Pro feel like acceleration, not ransom
- keep Agency focused on rights and business value, not random feature stuffing

## Launch pricing FAQ notes
- if conversion is weaker than expected, improve proof and packaging before discounting
- do not start with lifetime unlimited support promises
- consider a limited launch bonus instead of reducing the list price

## Future options
- yearly updates add-on
- premium support add-on
- module packs
- consulting or setup session upsell
